# Image shot-list — What Plants Need (Grade 3)

## 1. `pl-img-seed-to-plant` — attach to "The Quiet Life of a Seed" (pl-reading)
**Prompt:** A simple, cheerful four-stage picture for 3rd graders showing a seed growing: a seed
in soil, a root going down, a green stem coming up, and a small leafy plant in the sun. Label
the four needs around it with tiny icons: a water drop, a sun, air swirls, and a warm
thermometer. Bright and friendly.
**Alt text:** "Four stages of a seed growing: a seed in soil, then a root growing down, then a
green stem rising up, then a small leafy plant in the sun. Around it are four icons for its
needs: a water drop, a sun, swirls of air, and a warm thermometer."

## 2. `pl-img-dark-vs-light` — optional, attach to the quiz
**Prompt:** Two potted plants side by side: one in bright sunlight, green and healthy; one in a
dark closet, pale and droopy. Both have water. Caption: 'both watered, only one has light.'
Simple and clear for young kids.
**Alt text:** "Two potted plants, both watered. The one in bright sunlight is green and healthy.
The one in a dark closet is pale and droopy, showing a plant needs light, not just water."
