ABOUT ALLOFLOW DESKTOP (BETA)
=============================

WHAT THIS IS

AlloFlow helps educators turn any material into accessible, differentiated
learning resources: leveled readers, glossaries, quizzes, visual supports,
110+ interactive STEM tools, SEL activities, and live classroom sessions
students join from their own devices.

The Desktop edition is local-first by design:

  * Live classroom sessions run on YOUR computer and your school's network.
    Student work does not pass through any cloud service by default.
  * Optional built-in AI runs entirely on this device: a local text engine,
    local voices (Kokoro), and a local image generator - no accounts needed.
  * Cloud AI (for example Gemini) is used only if you add your own key.

AlloFlow is designed to support school-controlled, privacy-first
deployments. Whether a deployment meets FERPA or district policy also
depends on your institution's configuration and agreements.

ABOUT THE "UNKNOWN PUBLISHER" / SMARTSCREEN WARNING

Windows may have warned you that this installer is from an "unknown
publisher". That is expected for this beta: AlloFlow is not yet signed
with a paid code-signing certificate. Signing requires a commercial
identity-verification process that is planned before wide release.
Until then, Windows shows its standard caution for any unsigned app.
If you obtained this installer directly from AlloFlow's repository or
from the person who built it for you, you can proceed with
"More info" -> "Run anyway".
