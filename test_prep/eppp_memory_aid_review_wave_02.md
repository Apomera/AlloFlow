# EPPP memory-aid review wave 02

Status: source and editorial review complete; independent qualified expert review and production validation pending.

This balanced wave rewrites 16 additional legacy aids - two per EPPP domain. Stable legacy IDs prevent same-title duplicates from being promoted accidentally. The review removes deterministic, stigmatizing, legally universal, psychometrically invalid, and clinically unsafe shortcuts.

| Domain | Memory aid | Editorial outcome | Sources |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Brain Lobes & Functions | Replaced one-lobe/one-function localization with a network-aware retrieval map. | [Physiology, Brain](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/sites/books/NBK551718/) |
| 1 | Broca's vs. Wernicke's Aphasia | Removed stigmatizing language, intact-comprehension claims, and single-spot localization. | [The neuroanatomy of Broca's aphasia](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC12928379/); [Wernicke Aphasia](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK441951/) |
| 2 | Classical vs. Operant Conditioning | Replaced the passive/active dichotomy with stimulus-stimulus versus response-consequence relations. | [Psychology 2e, Section 6.2: Classical Conditioning](https://openstax.org/books/psychology-2e/pages/6-2-classical-conditioning); [Psychology 2e, Section 6.3: Operant Conditioning](https://openstax.org/books/psychology-2e/pages/6-3-operant-conditioning) |
| 2 | Reinforcement Schedules | Corrected interval delivery, removed universal highest/most-resistant claims, and bounded laboratory patterns. | [Psychology 2e, Section 6.3: Operant Conditioning](https://openstax.org/books/psychology-2e/pages/6-3-operant-conditioning) |
| 3 | Milgram, Asch, Zimbardo | Preserved the study distinctions while adding exact context and rejecting the automatic-role conclusion. | [Psychology 2e, Section 12.4: Conformity, Compliance, and Obedience](https://openstax.org/books/psychology-2e/pages/12-4-conformity-compliance-and-obedience); [Behavioral Study of Obedience](https://doi.org/10.1037/h0040525); [Rethinking the nature of cruelty: The role of identity leadership in the Stanford Prison Experiment](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/31380665/) |
| 3 | Bystander Effect & Diffusion of Responsibility | Replaced an absolute rule with the meta-analytic effect, moderators, and a safe action cue. | [The bystander-effect: a meta-analytic review on bystander intervention in dangerous and non-dangerous emergencies](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21534650/) |
| 4 | Attachment Types | Removed caregiver-blaming analogies and reframed categories as relationship-specific observations. | [Infant-parent attachment: Definition, types, antecedents, measurement and outcome](https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC2724160/) |
| 4 | Kohlberg's Moral Development | Retained the six-stage cue while removing age stereotypes and universal-development claims. | [Psychology 2e, Section 9.2: Lifespan Theories](https://openstax.org/books/psychology-2e/pages/9-2-lifespan-theories) |
| 5 | Standard Score Conversions | Removed the false EPPP 500/100 scale and made every conversion conditional on the manual and reference distribution. | [NCME Glossary of Important Assessment and Measurement Terms](https://ncme.org/resources-publications/professional-learning/glossary); [Standards for Educational and Psychological Testing (2014)](https://www.testingstandards.net/uploads/7/6/6/4/76643089/standards_2014edition.pdf) |
| 5 | Test Bias & Fairness | Separated DIF from bias, expanded fairness beyond prediction, and removed universal legal and culture-free claims. | [Standards for Educational and Psychological Testing (2014)](https://www.testingstandards.net/uploads/7/6/6/4/76643089/standards_2014edition.pdf); [NAEP Technical Documentation: Differential Item Functioning](https://nces.ed.gov/nationsreportcard/tdw/analysis/scaling_checks_dif.aspx) |
| 6 | Exposure & Response Prevention (ERP) | Replaced coercive blocking and mandatory habituation with collaborative, inhibitory-learning-aware ERP. | [Obsessive-compulsive disorder and body dysmorphic disorder: treatment (CG31)](https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/cg31); [Maximizing exposure therapy: An inhibitory learning approach](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.brat.2014.04.006) |
| 6 | Suicide Risk Assessment | Removed a poorly predictive, stereotype-prone score and replaced it with NIMH's direct multifactorial assessment structure. | [Adult Outpatient Brief Suicide Safety Assessment Guide](https://www.nimh.nih.gov/research/research-conducted-at-nimh/asq-toolkit-materials/adult-outpatient/adult-outpatient-brief-suicide-safety-assessment-guide); [Risk and Protective Factors for Suicide](https://www.cdc.gov/suicide/risk-factors/) |
| 7 | Threats to Internal Validity | Reorganized the mnemonic around causal alternatives and corrected the claim that randomization fixes most threats. | [What Works Clearinghouse Procedures and Standards Handbook, Version 5.0](https://ies.ed.gov/ncee/wwc/Handbooks) |
| 7 | Statistical Power | Corrected the probability statement, bounded .80, and removed observed-power and real-effect interpretations. | [A power primer](https://doi.org/10.1037/0033-2909.112.1.155); [The ASA Statement on p-Values: Context, Process, and Purpose](https://www.amstat.org/asa/files/pdfs/p-valuestatement.pdf) |
| 8 | Ethical Decision-Making Steps | Reframed IDEAL as a nonexclusive reasoning aid and added roles, authority conflicts, privacy, equity, monitoring, and accountability. | [Ethical Principles of Psychologists and Code of Conduct](https://www.apa.org/ethics/code) |
| 8 | Multiple Relationships (Dual) | Used the Code definition and impairment/exploitation test while correcting the after-two-years-is-allowed shortcut. | [Ethical Principles of Psychologists and Code of Conduct](https://www.apa.org/ethics/code) |
