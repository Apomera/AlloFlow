Joining a Live Session

1️⃣
Go to the URL

Open the link your teacher gives you. It might be on the board, in Google Classroom, or shared in a message.

2️⃣
Enter the Code

Type the 4-letter code your teacher puts on the board — like ABCD. Letters only, no numbers.

3️⃣
Pick a Codename

Choose a fun codename (or your teacher assigns one). Your real name is never used. You're in — let's go! 🎮

Live Session Modes
⚔️ Boss Battle

The whole class works together. Correct answers deal damage to the boss!

🏆 Team Showdown

Reading Helpers

If reading is hard, these options in the Settings ⚙️ menu can make a big difference.

Bigger Text

Makes all words larger so they're easier to read on any screen.

Read Aloud

Click any sentence and it reads it to you. Works in 40+ languages, no internet needed.

Bionic Mode

Bolds the first part of each word to help your eyes track across the line faster.

Color Overlay

Changes the background to Peach, Blue, or Yellow — can help with visual stress (Irlen syndrome).

Dyslexia Font

Switches to OpenDyslexic, Lexend, or Atkinson Hyperlegible — typefaces designed to reduce letter confusion.

Reading Ruler

A horizontal bar follows your mouse to keep your place on the line you're reading.

Dark Mode

Full dark theme — easier on the eyes in low-light classrooms or at night.

Voice Dictation

Speak your answer instead of typing. Works on all question and writing fields.

Line Focus

Dims all text except the sentence you're on — helps with focus and attention.

Adventure Mode 🗡️

🎲
Your choices shape the story

Every decision branches the plot. Different choices lead to different endings — there's no one right path.

💰
Spend gold wisely

Buy weapons, healing potions, or magic items from the in-game store. Save up for the big purchases.

🎲
Dice rolls add luck

Some actions use 3D dice. Higher rolls = better outcomes. Level up your character to improve your odds.

🤝
Democracy Mode

In live sessions, the whole class votes on which path to take. Majority wins — choose wisely!

📖
Export your story

When you finish, your teacher can export your entire adventure as a storybook to keep!

🧠
It's about the reading

The story is built from your actual lesson content. The more you read, the better your choices get.

Vocabulary Games

These games are generated from the vocabulary words in your lesson. Every game is different each time!

Memory Match

Flip cards to match words with their definitions. Faster matches = more XP.

Word Search

Find vocabulary words hidden in a grid. Click the first and last letter to highlight.

Crossword

Fill in the boxes using the clues. Works with your keyboard — no mouse needed.

Bingo

Listen to definitions and mark your card. First to get five in a row wins!

Word Scramble

Unscramble the letters to spell the vocabulary word. Hint button available if you're stuck.

Flashcards

Flip through word + definition cards at your own pace. Click to flip, arrow keys to navigate.

Word Sounds 👂

Phonemic awareness drills — practice hearing and working with the sounds in words. 8 different activity types.

Counting

How many sounds are in "cat"? → 3

Find Sounds

Identify the first, middle, or last sound in a word.

Blend Sounds

/k/ /æ/ /t/ → what word? → "cat"

Break Apart

"dog" → /d/ /ɒ/ /g/ — use the Elkonin boxes to segment each sound.

Rhyme Time

What rhymes with "hat"? — bat, cat, mat...

Sound Mapping

Match sounds to their written letters (grapheme-phoneme bridge).

Sight & Spell

Orthography and spelling practice with adaptive difficulty.

Word Families

Group words by their rime: -at, -ing, -ock...

💡
Tips: Listen to the whole word before answering. Use the Elkonin boxes — one box per sound. Get 3 correct in a row for a streak bonus!

Something Not Working?

Refresh the page

Most issues are fixed by pressing F5 or Ctrl+R to reload.

Check your internet

If the page loads but content doesn't appear, check that you're connected.

Audio not playing

Click anywhere on the page first — browsers require a user gesture before playing audio.

Stuck in a game

Press Esc to close the current activity and return to the main view.

Ask AlloBot

Click the 🤖 icon anywhere in the app — AlloBot knows what tool you're using and can help.

Ask your teacher

If refreshing and AlloBot don't fix it, your teacher can reset or resend your session.
