# Image shot-list — Body Systems (Grade 6)

## 1. `bs-img-systems-relay` — attach to "The Relay Race" (bs-reading)
**Prompt:** A friendly diagram tracing one oxygen molecule through the body: lungs (respiratory)
to a labeled arrow into the blood, to the heart and vessels (circulatory), to a muscle cell,
with the digestive system feeding nutrients into the blood and the brain (nervous) shown
sending a signal. Each system a different color, clear labels, not gory.
**Alt text:** "A diagram tracing oxygen through the body. It enters the lungs, labeled
respiratory, passes into the blood, and the heart and vessels, labeled circulatory, carry it to
a muscle cell. The digestive system adds nutrients to the blood, and the brain, labeled nervous
system, sends a signal."

## 2. `bs-img-exercise-teamwork` — optional, attach to the quiz
**Prompt:** A two-panel illustration of a child resting versus sprinting. In the sprint panel,
the lungs and heart are both highlighted with 'faster' arrows, showing the respiratory and
circulatory systems speeding up together. Friendly, educational.
**Alt text:** "Two panels of a child. At rest, the lungs and heart are calm. While sprinting,
both the lungs and heart are highlighted with faster arrows, showing the breathing and
circulatory systems speed up together."
