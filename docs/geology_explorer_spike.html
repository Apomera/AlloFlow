Geology Explorer

A voxel cross-section of the crust. Drag to orbit, scroll to zoom. Click a block to identify it; slice or excavate to read the structure.

SPIKE — value test, not production

Explore

Identify (default): click any block.
Slice: peel away the front to see the layers + intrusion in cross-section.
Excavate: dig top-down — you must remove the layers above first (law of superposition).
Click a block to identify the rock — its type, how it formed, its depth, and its age relationship to its neighbours.
