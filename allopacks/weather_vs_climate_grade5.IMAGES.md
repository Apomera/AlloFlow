## 1. `wx-img-mood-personality` — attach to "Mood and Personality" (wx-reading)
**Prompt:** A friendly two-panel comparison for kids. Left panel labeled "Weather — today's
mood": one day on a calendar with a rain cloud. Right panel labeled "Climate — the
personality": a thirty-year span shown as many small weather icons averaging into a mostly
sunny pattern. Flat educational illustration, clear labels.
**Alt text:** "Two panels comparing weather and climate. The left panel, labeled weather,
shows a single calendar day with a rain cloud. The right panel, labeled climate, shows thirty
years of many small weather icons that average out to a mostly sunny pattern."

## 2. `wx-img-graph-trend` — optional, attach to the quiz
**Prompt:** A simple line graph for 5th graders showing jagged yearly temperature dots with a
smooth upward trend line drawn through them, labeled "one year = weather" pointing at a dot and
"the trend = climate" pointing at the line. Clean, two colors, large labels.
**Alt text:** "A line graph of yearly temperatures. Individual year dots jump up and down; a
smooth line through them rises gradually. A label points at one dot reading one year equals
weather, and another points at the line reading the trend equals climate."
