# Image shot-list — States of Matter (Grade 4-5)

## 1. `sm-img-particle-states` — attach to "The Dance Floor" (sm-reading)
**Prompt:** Three labeled boxes side by side showing particle arrangement for 4th graders.
Solid: dots packed in a neat grid with tiny vibration marks. Liquid: dots close together but
loosely arranged with small motion arrows. Gas: dots far apart with long motion arrows filling
the whole box. Bright, simple, one color per state.
**Alt text:** "Three boxes showing particles. In the solid box, dots are packed in a tight grid
with small vibration marks. In the liquid box, dots are close but loosely arranged with short
motion arrows. In the gas box, dots are spread far apart with long motion arrows."

## 2. `sm-img-boiling-gap` — optional, attach to the quiz
**Prompt:** A kettle with a spout, showing a small CLEAR gap right at the spout labeled
"invisible water vapor (the real gas)" and the white cloud beginning slightly further away
labeled "tiny liquid droplets you can see." Simple, clear labels.
**Alt text:** "A kettle spout with a small clear gap labeled invisible water vapor, and the
white cloud beginning slightly farther away labeled tiny liquid droplets you can see."
