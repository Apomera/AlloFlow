# Image shot-list — Food Webs (Grade 5)

## 1. `ec-img-food-web` — attach to "Pull One Thread" (ec-reading)
**Prompt:** A colorful meadow food web for 5th graders with arrows showing energy flow: grass
and wildflowers at the base; grasshopper, rabbit, and mouse above; snake and hawk at the top;
mushrooms and an earthworm along the bottom labeled decomposers, with arrows returning to the
soil. Every arrow points in the direction the energy travels. Flat educational illustration.
**Alt text:** "A meadow food web. Arrows lead from grass and wildflowers to a grasshopper,
rabbit, and mouse, then onward to a snake and a hawk. Along the bottom, mushrooms and an
earthworm labeled decomposers have arrows returning nutrients to the soil."

## 2. `ec-img-energy-pyramid` — optional, attach to the anchor chart
**Prompt:** An energy pyramid with four levels, each visibly about one tenth the size of the
one below: many grass plants at the base, fewer grasshoppers, fewer mice, one hawk at the top,
labeled "about 10% moves up each level."
**Alt text:** "An energy pyramid. A wide base of grass plants supports a smaller band of
grasshoppers, then a smaller band of mice, then a single hawk at the narrow top, labeled about
ten percent of energy moves up each level."

## 3. `ec-img-yellowstone` — optional, attach to the quiz
**Prompt:** A two-panel comparison of a river valley: on the left, without wolves — heavy elk
grazing, bare riverbanks, few young trees. On the right, with wolves — willows and aspens
growing, a beaver dam, songbirds. Educational, hopeful, not gory.
**Alt text:** "Two panels of the same river valley. Without wolves, elk graze heavily and the
banks are bare. With wolves, young willows and aspens grow, a beaver dam sits in the stream,
and songbirds perch nearby."
