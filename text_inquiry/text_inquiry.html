1. Identify the source

Record enough edition and context information for another reader to locate the same text. Use public, licensed, or teacher-approved material.

2. Analyze a bounded text

Paste up to 50,000 characters. The text is held only in memory and is not included in Research Hub captures or downloads.

Privacy boundary: do not paste private interviews, student records, direct identifiers, unpublished community testimony, or restricted/copyrighted text you are not permitted to process.

3. Inspect patterns, then return to passages

Lexical diversity is a descriptive ratio of unique words to all words in this bounded sample. It is not a score of writing quality.
