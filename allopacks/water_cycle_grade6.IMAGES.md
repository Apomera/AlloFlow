# Image shot-list — Water Cycle flagship pack

Generate each in-app (Image tool) or with any generator, then attach where noted. Alt text is
written to born-accessible standards — use it verbatim.

## 1. `wc-img-cycle-diagram` — attach to "Journey of a Raindrop" (wc-reading)
**Prompt:** A clean, labeled educational diagram of the water cycle for 6th graders: the Sun
over an ocean on the left, arrows showing evaporation rising, a cloud forming (condensation),
rain falling on a mountain (precipitation), a river running back to the ocean (runoff/
collection), and a tree with small arrows from its leaves (transpiration). Flat colorful
illustration style, large clear labels, no photorealism.
**Alt text:** "Diagram of the water cycle. The Sun heats the ocean; an arrow labeled
evaporation rises to a cloud labeled condensation; rain labeled precipitation falls on a
mountain; a river labeled runoff and collection flows back to the ocean; a tree shows arrows
labeled transpiration rising from its leaves."

## 2. `wc-img-cloud-droplets` — optional, attach to the FAQ or quiz
**Prompt:** A friendly zoomed-in illustration showing a cloud is made of billions of tiny
floating water droplets — a magnifying circle over a cartoon cloud revealing individual
droplets. Educational, warm, simple.
**Alt text:** "A magnifying glass over a cloud reveals it is made of many tiny floating water
droplets."

## 3. `wc-img-glass-condensation` — optional, attach to concept-sort intro
**Prompt:** A cold glass of iced water on a table with visible condensation droplets forming
on the outside, warm kitchen background, simple educational illustration.
**Alt text:** "A cold glass of iced water with water droplets forming on its outside surface —
condensation from the surrounding air."
