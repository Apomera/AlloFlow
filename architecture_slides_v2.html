Equity First

Universal Access

AlloFlow guarantees that every student—regardless of internet access at home—receives full pedagogical support.

100% Offline Capability
Once loaded, AlloFlow runs entirely inside Chrome as a self-contained application. There are no external databases, allowing schools to bridge the digital divide seamlessly.
Differentiation

Instant Leveled Text

A teacher's most scarce resource is time. Adapting a single reading passage for five different reading levels previously took hours of tedious manual rewriting.

Multi-Lexile
Generate custom materials from Kindergarten to 12th grade complexity in seconds.
Multilingual Support
Automatically translates materials to serve ELL and neurodivergent populations side-by-side.
Engagement

Multi-Modal Learning

One size never fits all. AlloFlow transforms dry standards into multiple interactive formats instantly, targeting diverse learning styles and IEP requirements.

Auto-Generated Activities
Turn a single PDF or topic into engaging Escape Rooms, Bingo, Crosswords, Concept Sorts, and 3D Adventure games to reach students who struggle with traditional worksheets.
Science of Reading

Phonemic Precision

Teaching reading remotely or independently is historically difficult because English spelling frequently violates spoken rules.

Word Sounds Studio
AlloFlow bridges the gap between visual letters (orthography) and auditory sounds (IPA). It automatically splits words into pure, un-accented phonemes for isolation, blending, and rhyming practice.
Scaffolding

Interactive Guidance

Allobot isn't just a chatbot that spits out answers. It is an embodied pedagogical agent designed to guide students toward their own "aha!" moments.

The Tour Engine
When a student needs help, Allobot physically interacts with the application—pointing at buttons, opening sidebars, and speaking instructions out loud to guide learners visually and auditorily.
FERPA Compliance

Absolute Data Privacy

We believe student data should never be collected, sold, or stored on corporate servers. AlloFlow solves data privacy by eliminating backend databases entirely.

Local Persistence
All quiz scores, XP, and progress points are heavily compressed and stored explicitly inside the district's local browser context. Data stays inside the classroom walls.
Community

Empowering Families

RTI (Response to Intervention) thrives when families are involved, but educational software is often gatekept by thousands of dollars in licensing fees.

At-Home Practice
Because AlloFlow has a $0 software cost, parents can access the exact same Tier-3 reading and math interventions at the kitchen table that teachers use in the classroom.
Mission

The Open-Source Commitment

Built by a public school educator to dismantle the "Differentiation Tax"—the unacceptable reality that teachers must spend their own money to support diverse learners.

Free Forever
AlloFlow is completely open-source. Anyone can use, modify, and distribute it to ensure every student receives equitable, individualized instruction.
