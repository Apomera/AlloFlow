        First-time setup
        
              Choose your AI
              
        
              AlloFlow uses an AI engine to create lessons, read aloud, and answer questions. Pick how you'd like it to work — you can change this any time under ⚙ Settings → AI.
              
      
              

      
      
              
        
        
        
        
              
          
        
              
      
              

      
      
              
        
                
          
              1. Open Google AI Studio ↗ — it opens in your web browser.
                2. 
          
              2. Sign in with any Google account (a school or personal Gmail both work).
                3. 
          
              3. Click “Create API key”, then click the copy button next to the new key.
                4. 
          
              4. Come back here and paste it below.
                5. 
        
              
        
        
        
              The key is stored only on this computer, in protected storage. The free tier is enough for everyday classroom use.
              
        
              
          
          
        
              
      
              

      
      
              
        
                
          
              • ✅ Private: lessons and student text never leave this computer.
                • 
          
              • 📥 One-time download: a starter AI model (Qwen 2.5, about 2 GB). Keep the app open until it finishes.
                • 
          
              • 🖥️ Hardware: works best with 8 GB+ memory and ~2.5 GB free disk space. On older computers it runs, just more slowly.
                • 
          
              • ⏱️ Speed: answers take a bit longer than cloud AI — that's the privacy trade.
