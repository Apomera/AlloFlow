## What We Offer Knowbility

1. **A free tool to recommend to education clients** — When districts say "we can't afford $5-25/page," Knowbility can point them to AlloFlow's pipeline for the ~70-80% of simple documents, reserving Knowbility's expert services for the complex 20-30%.

2. **An AccessU session** — "When AI Meets axe-core" workshop (draft proposal ready). Could be co-presented with Knowbility staff.

3. **An open-source reference implementation** — AlloFlow's axe-core integration, self-healing loop, and VPAT are all open source. Knowbility can point developers to the codebase.

4. **Education sector impact at scale** — AlloFlow serves K-12 and higher ed, where Knowbility's mission is most needed but budgets are smallest.

5. **Compliance portfolio report** — NEW: The batch pipeline now generates a comprehensive WCAG compliance evidence document suitable for institutional review. This could be a template Knowbility recommends to clients.

---

## What We Need from Knowbility

1. **AccessWorks usability testing** — Native screen reader users navigating the actual interface. No amount of automated testing replaces this.

2. **Expert manual audit** — The VPAT has 11 criteria marked "Partially Supports (pending verification)." Human expert review would resolve these.

3. **VPAT/ACR validation** — A Knowbility-validated Accessibility Conformance Report would transform AlloFlow's institutional credibility.

"I noticed Knowbility already has a K-12 Digital Accessibility program — the Access Toolkit, the K-12 Summit, the community network. AlloFlow is built specifically for K-12. I'm starting as a school psychologist at Portland Public Schools next week. Portland falls under the April 24th deadline as a 50,000+ entity. This isn't abstract for me — it's my district, my students, my colleagues' documents."

### The Title II Urgency
"April 24th is 22 days away. Every school district in the country is facing the same problem: thousands of inaccessible documents and a budget that can't cover professional remediation at scale. AlloFlow's pipeline isn't a replacement for Knowbility's expertise — it's a force multiplier. Handle the 70% automatically, so your team can focus on the 30% that actually needs human judgment."

### The Demo Offer
"I'd love to do a live demo — upload a real PDF, watch the multi-auditor analysis, see the axe-core verification, look at the compliance report that comes out. It takes about 3 minutes per document. I think once you see it in action, the partnership conversation becomes very concrete."

### The AccessU Pitch
"I've drafted a session proposal for AccessU: 'When AI Meets axe-core.' It covers both the technical pipeline and an honest assessment of what AI can and can't do. I'd love to co-present with someone from Knowbility — that would give the session credibility and demonstrate the partnership model."

### If They Ask About Cost
"I have no budget — I'm a first-year school psychologist. But I'm not asking for charity. I'm proposing a mission partnership where both organizations benefit. AlloFlow stays free and open source. Knowbility gets a tool to recommend, a case study to publish, and a conference session to present. If some form of discounted audit is possible, that would be incredible — but the partnership has value even without it."

### Closing
"The students who need accessible documents most are the ones whose schools can least afford to create them. That's the problem both of us are trying to solve. I think we solve it better together."

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| They see AlloFlow as competition to their consulting | Frame as complementary: "We handle the simple 70%, you handle the expert 30%." The pipeline explicitly flags documents for expert review. |
| They're too busy with leadership transition | Respect the timing. Offer low-commitment starting points (demo, CFP co-submission). Don't push for a formal partnership agreement until after Jillian settles in. |
| The audit is too expensive even with discount | Push for the "case study" model: Knowbility does the audit as research/content, publishes findings, both benefit. |
| AccessU proposal rejected | Resubmit as shorter presentation. Or attend as general participant and network. |
| Pipeline has a bad day during demo | Always have a pre-recorded backup demo. Test with their specific document types before the meeting. |
