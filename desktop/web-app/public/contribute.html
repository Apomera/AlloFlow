Contribute a Lesson or Tool

Share a student-authored lesson or educator-built STEM Lab tool with the AlloFlow community. Submissions are reviewed before public listing. By default, submissions are anonymous; you can opt in to a credit line.

1. Paste or upload your JSON

If you saved a lesson or tool from inside AlloFlow, you should have a .json file. Drag it in, click to upload, or paste the contents below.

Schema: top-level payload object plus metadata (title, subject, grade_level, etc.). The validator will tell you if anything is missing.

2. Metadata

A few fields the catalog needs for browsing and filtering.

A range or single grade. Use Pre-K, K, or numbers.

By default, submissions are anonymous. You may add a short credit line. Do not include full names of minors.

3. Privacy scan

Before submitting, we scan your JSON for patterns that look like personally identifiable information (names, emails, phone numbers, addresses). The scan runs entirely in your browser.

After submission, an AlloFlow maintainer will review and either publish to the public catalog or reach out with questions.
