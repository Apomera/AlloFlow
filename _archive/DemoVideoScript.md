## 3-Minute Extended Version

### INTRO (0-20 seconds)
**[Screen: You on camera or professional B-roll of classroom]**

"Hi, I'm Aaron Pomeranz. I'm a school psychologist, and I spent years watching teachers struggle with the same problem: differentiation takes hours, and the tools that help cost thousands of dollars."

"So I built AlloFlow—an open-source platform that does what six different subscriptions do, for free."

---

### DEMO SECTION 1: Content Generation (20-60 seconds)
**[Screen recording with voiceover]**

"Let me show you. I'll paste an article about the water cycle."

**[Paste text, click Generate]**

"One click gives me three reading levels—below grade, on grade, above grade. Each one is properly differentiated, not just vocabulary-swapped."

**[Show compare toggle]**

"I can compare them side by side to see exactly what changed."

**[Click Glossary]**

"The glossary automatically pulls Tier 2 and Tier 3 vocabulary, adds visual icons, and includes audio pronunciation."

---

### DEMO SECTION 2: Gamification (60-100 seconds)
**[Screen recording]**

"But here's where it gets interesting. That same content can become..."

**[Click Escape Room]**
"An escape room with ciphers and puzzles..."

**[Click Adventure Mode]**
"A choose-your-own-adventure story where students explore the water cycle as a narrative..."

**[Click Boss Battle]**
"Or a whole-class boss battle where correct answers do damage."

**[Show XP and level system]**

"Students earn XP, level up, and buy items in a shop. They think they're playing a game. They're actually mastering content."

---

### DEMO SECTION 3: Accessibility (100-130 seconds)
**[Screen recording]**

"Now, accessibility."

**[Toggle Bionic Reading]**
"Bionic reading—bolds the first part of each word to guide the eye."

**[Toggle OpenDyslexic font]**
"Dyslexia-friendly fonts."

**[Toggle color overlay]**
"Color overlays for Irlen syndrome."

**[Click TTS button, show highlighting]**
"Text-to-speech with word highlighting."

"These aren't add-ons. They're built into every view."

---

### DEMO SECTION 4: Phonics & ORF (130-160 seconds)
**[Screen recording]**

"For elementary teachers—Word Sounds."

**[Show phoneme counting activity]**
"Eight phonemic awareness activities generated from your vocabulary list."

**[Show Elkonin boxes]**
"Elkonin boxes for segmentation. Rhyme matching. First sound isolation."

**[Show ORF recording]**
"And oral reading fluency assessment with WCPM scoring and color-coded feedback. This usually costs $15-50 per student. Here, it's included."

---

## Shot List for Video Production

| Timestamp | Shot Type | Content |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| 0:00-0:05 | Motion graphics | Competitor logos → AlloFlow logo |
| 0:05-0:15 | B-roll or animation | Teacher overwhelmed by tabs |
| 0:15-0:45 | Screen recording | Quick feature montage |
| 0:45-0:60 | Screen + text overlay | GitHub page, URL, tagline |

### Equipment Needed
- Screen recording software (OBS, Loom, or Camtasia)
- Microphone (USB condenser or lapel mic)
- Simple motion graphics (Canva, After Effects, or Kapwing)

### Music
- Upbeat but not distracting
- Royalty-free (Epidemic Sound, Artlist, or YouTube Audio Library)

---

*Record the screen demo first, then write the voiceover to match the pacing. Aim for 120-140 words per minute for comfortable listening.*
