# Image shot-list — Central Idea and Evidence (Grade 7)

## 1. `ci-img-claim-evidence` — attach to "More Than One Thread" (ci-reading)
**Prompt:** A clean diagram for middle schoolers showing a claim box at the top ('Bike lanes make
streets safer') with two arrows down to two evidence boxes ('40% fewer injuries in a study',
'more people bike to work'), and a third box off to the side labeled 'true but not relevant'
('the lanes are painted green') with a dashed, faded arrow. Flat educational style.
**Alt text:** "A claim box reading bike lanes make streets safer connects with solid arrows to
two evidence boxes: forty percent fewer injuries in a study, and more people bike to work. A
separate faded box reads the lanes are painted green, labeled true but not relevant."

## 2. `ci-img-two-threads` — optional, attach to the anchor chart
**Prompt:** An illustration of a single article page with two colored highlighter threads woven
through it, one tracing a central idea about science and the other tracing a central idea about
community, showing both run through the whole text.
**Alt text:** "An article page with two colored highlighted threads woven through the
paragraphs, one following a science idea and the other a community idea, showing a text can
carry two central ideas at once."
