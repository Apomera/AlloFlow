# Image shot-list — Main Idea (Grade 4-5)

## 1. `mi-img-suitcase` — attach to "The Suitcase Test" (mi-reading)
**Prompt:** A friendly illustration of an open suitcase labeled MAIN IDEA, with folded clothes
inside each labeled as a supporting detail, and one odd item (a rubber duck) sitting outside
the suitcase labeled "interesting, but does not belong." Warm, kid-friendly, clear labels.
**Alt text:** "An open suitcase labeled main idea holds several folded items, each labeled as a
supporting detail. Outside the suitcase sits a rubber duck labeled interesting but does not
belong."

## 2. `mi-img-topic-vs-idea` — optional, attach to the anchor chart
**Prompt:** A simple two-column comparison. Left column headed TOPIC shows single words: dogs,
volcanoes, bees. Right column headed MAIN IDEA shows full sentences about each. Clean, bold
type, plenty of white space.
**Alt text:** "A two-column comparison. The left column, headed topic, lists single words such
as dogs, volcanoes, and bees. The right column, headed main idea, gives a full sentence about
each one."
